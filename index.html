01 — AboutBiografía

A career built on trust, executed with discipline. Una carrera construida sobre la confianza, ejecutada con disciplina.

A "Mustang" officer who rose from the enlisted ranks, Santos brings the rare combination of NCO-grade execution and field-grade strategic judgment to every program he leads. Oficial "Mustang" que ascendió desde las filas de tropa, Santos aporta la rara combinación de la ejecución de un suboficial con el juicio estratégico de un oficial superior a cada programa que dirige.

Santos H. Arroyo Claudio is a Distinguished Leadership Graduate of the U.S. Army Officer Candidate School, a retired Major, and a project management professional with two decades of experience delivering complex programs in regulated, high-stakes environments. A native of Vega Alta, Puerto Rico, he began his Army service in 1991 as an enlisted Soldier and was commissioned a Second Lieutenant in 2000 — a "Mustang" path that defines how he leads today.

His military career spans deployments to Bosnia-Herzegovina, Kuwait, and Iraq, including service as Deputy Chief of a Stability Transition Team advising the Iraqi Border Patrol, and command of more than 400 Soldiers at Fort Hood as Commander of HHC, 3rd Brigade Special Troops Battalion. In each role he was responsible not only for mission outcomes but for the people, equipment, and accountability that made those outcomes possible.

In the civilian sector he has carried that same standard into IT project management — leading classified network deployments at Army Futures Command, integrating a Student Information System across eleven institutions at Texas A&M University-Central Texas, and delivering a $3M Remote Operations Control Center for SunPower Corporation under strict NERC reliability standards. Today he runs Major Business Solutions, LLC, advising clients on digital transformation, process optimization, and low-code Power Platform solutions — including a current engagement with the Cooperativa Hidroeléctrica de la Montaña in Puerto Rico.
